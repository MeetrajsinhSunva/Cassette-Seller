Genres

A music genre can be defined as a conventional category that groups together pieces of music sharing a set of conventions or traditions. Music is divided into different genres using a range of criteria.

JAZZ RAP

Jazz is characterized by swing and blue notes, complex chords, call and response vocals, polyrhythms and improvisation. Jazz has roots in European harmony and African rhythmic rituals. Late 19th century, New Orleans, U.S.

BOOM-BAP

Boom bap is a subgenre of the late 1980s and 1990s “golden age” of hip-hop that focuses on snappy, vintage-sounding drum hits programmed with funky swing, jazzy musical influences, and smooth bass lines.

OLD-SKOOL

Old school is easily identified by its relatively simple raps -- most lines take up approximately equal amounts of time, and the rhythms of the language rarely twisted around the beats of the song with retro music.

hip hop

Hip-hop, cultural movement that attained widespread popularity in the 1980s and ’90s and also the backing music for rap, the musical style incorporating rhythmic and/or rhyming speech that become melodious.

Trend Setters

McFly-Young Dumb Thrills

Young Dumb Thrills is the sixth studio album from English pop rock band McFly. Their first studio album since 2010, the album was released on 13 November 2020, and is their first under record label BMG.[2] The album debuted at number 2 in the UK selling 20,750 in its first week.[3] It became the band's highest charting album since Wonderland in 2005.

Lany- Mama's Boy

The indie-pop dreamboats are back! In 2017, LANY rose to fame pretty quickly after their self-titled debut album launched them right into multiple sold-out tours with crazy international success. Then in 2018, they tore us apart with an incredibly vulnerable portrait of love, heartbreak, and grief on their sophomore album, Malibu Nights. Today, the LA-based trio release their highly-anticipated third studio album, mama’s boy. As heartthrob frontman, Paul Jason Klein puts it perfectly in a tweet, “broke our hearts making malibu nights broke our backs making mama’s boy.”

Awesome Mix 2

Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 2: Awesome Mix Vol. 2 (Original Motion Picture Soundtrack) is the soundtrack album for the Marvel Studios film Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 2. Featuring the songs present on Peter Quill's mixtape in the film, the album was released by Hollywood Records on April 21, 2017. A separate film score album, Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 2 (Original Score), composed by Tyler Bates, was also released by Hollywood Records on the same date.
